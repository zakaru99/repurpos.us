How to use the ontology tree
  • Fit the tree to your window
    • Click and drag the tree to move it within your window
    • Zoom in and out using the scroll wheel on your mouse
  • Use the buttons in the upper left to toggle between data sources
  • Searches can be performed using the search box above the Tree
    • Begin typing and a list of options will appear
    • Select your search term and hit "enter" to initiate a search
  • Left click on compound nodes to open the drug profile page
  • Green nodes indicate a specific MoA
    • Right click green nodes to download all associated drugs
