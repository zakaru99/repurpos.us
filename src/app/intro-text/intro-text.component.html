reframedb

A screening library of 12,000 molecules assembled by combining three databases (Clarivate Integrity, GVK Excelra GoStar, and Citeline Pharmaprojects) to facilitate drug repurposing
How to use the database
  • Explore pharmaceutical and biological properties of individual compounds like imatinib
  • View screening data for all compounds which responded in a specific assay like Crypto-C. parvum proliferation assay (requires sign-in)
  • Search for similar structures to view pharmaceutical and biological annotation data and assay responses
